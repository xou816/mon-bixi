# Purpose of the Policy

The purpose of this Privacy Policy (the “Policy”) is to explain how your personal information is handled when you use the mon-bixi extension (the “Extension”) on https://secure.bixi.com (the “Site”). By using the Extension, you confirm your acceptance of this Policy. If you do not agree to this Policy, please do not use the Extension.

# What personal information do we collect? 

When you use the Extension on the Site, we collect information related to your Bixi trips during the year 2025, including the time of the trips and the departure and arrival points.

# How do we use your personal information?

We use your personal information primarily to provide you with the features of the Extension, i.e., to allow you to view a visual summary of your travel data.

# Do we use cookies?

We do not collect information through cookies and other tracking technologies. However, your travel data is stored in your browser and is only accessible by the Extension and the Site (“IndexedDB” technology). 

# Do we share your personal information?

No personal information is shared with third-party services or partners. The information collected is only accessible locally, by the Extension and the Site, from your browser only.

# How long is your personal information stored?

The personal information collected and stored in your browser is kept for as long as you wish. You can delete this information at any time and at your own discretion.

# How do we protect the confidentiality of your personal information?

The personal information we collect or have access to is only accessible by the Extension and the Site, and only from your browser. We are in no way able to directly access your collected information.

# What are your rights?

You can delete the information collected in your browser at any time and independently.
